About this database

Chironomids are non-biting midges that inhabit various environments. In particular, Polypedilum vanderplanki is capable of enduring near-complete desiccation, a phenonemon known as anhydrobiosis. We have employed genomics to understand the molecular basis of anhydrobiosis, and this midgebase is the portal for our genome projects. We have a mirror site hosted at Japan or Russia. This site is currently under construction (Planned to open around May 2022).
